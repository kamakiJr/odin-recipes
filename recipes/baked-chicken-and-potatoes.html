Baked Chicken and Potatoes

Description

This baked chicken and potatoes recipe is a comforting and delicious meal, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 medium potatoes, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until well coated.
  3. Place the chicken thighs on a baking sheet and arrange the potatoes around them.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the potatoes are golden and crispy.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving, if desired.
