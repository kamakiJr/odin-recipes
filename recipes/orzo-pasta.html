Orzo Pasta

Orzo pasta is a type of pasta shaped like a large grain of rice. It is commonly used in soups, salads, and side dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orzo pasta
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the orzo pasta and cook, stirring frequently, until it is lightly toasted, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 10-12 minutes, or until the orzo is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve warm as a side dish or as a base for a main course.
